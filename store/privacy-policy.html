Privacy Policy

Meet Attendance Tracker · Effective March 15, 2026

All data stays in your browser

Overview

Meet Attendance Tracker is a Chrome extension that helps Google Meet hosts track attendance and engagement during video calls. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how data is handled.

Data We Collect

The extension collects the following information only while tracking is actively enabled by the host:

  • Attendance data: Participant names, join/leave times, and session duration.
  • Engagement data: Microphone unmute events (when participants speak) and emoji reactions.
  • Chat messages: Messages sent in the Google Meet chat panel during tracked sessions.
  • Meeting metadata: Meeting title, meeting code, start/end timestamps.

How Data Is Stored

All data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's built-in storage API (chrome.storage.local). No data is transmitted to, processed by, or stored on any external server, cloud service, or third-party system.

Google API Usage

The extension may use the Google Meet REST API to retrieve attendance information (join/leave times). This requires you to sign in with your Google account via OAuth 2.0. The extension requests only the meetings.space.readonly scope, which provides read-only access to meeting attendance records. Your Google credentials are handled entirely by Chrome's built-in identity system and are never accessed or stored by the extension.

What We Do NOT Do

  • We do not sell, share, or transmit your data to any third party.
  • We do not track your browsing activity outside of Google Meet.
  • We do not collect data when tracking is not actively enabled.
  • We do not access other participants' browsers, devices, or accounts.
  • We do not use analytics, advertising, or telemetry services.
  • We do not store or process audio, video, or screen content.

Permissions Explained

  • storage: Save session data locally in your browser.
  • activeTab / tabs: Detect when you are on a Google Meet page and inject the tracking UI.
  • identity: Authenticate with Google to access Meet attendance records via the API.
  • Host permission (meet.google.com): Operate only on Google Meet pages — no other websites.

Your Control

  • You choose when to start and stop tracking — nothing is automatic.
  • You can archive or delete individual sessions from the dashboard at any time.
  • Removing the extension deletes all locally stored data permanently.

Children's Privacy

This extension is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the revised version will be included in the extension update and the effective date above will change.
