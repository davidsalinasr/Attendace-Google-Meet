How We Handle Your Data

Data stays in your browser

What we collect

  • Attendance: Participant names, join/leave times, and duration (from the Google Meet API when available).
  • Engagement: When participants unmute (speak) and send reactions, as seen in your Meet tab.

Where it's stored

All data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's built-in storage. Nothing is uploaded to any server.

What we don't do

  • We never sell or share your data with third parties.
  • We never track your browsing outside of Google Meet.
  • We never access other participants' browsers or devices.

Your control

You can delete all stored data at any time by removing the extension. You choose when to start and stop tracking — nothing is automatic.
